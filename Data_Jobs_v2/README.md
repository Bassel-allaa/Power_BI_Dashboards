## Introduction


The data job market is often a cluttered maze, but Dashboard V2.0 is here to help you find the way. Designed specifically for anyone looking to land or swap roles, it uses actual 2024 job data to bring you clear insights on compensation and hiring trends. Get all the details on titles, salaries, and locations in one streamlined interface

## Skills Showcased

* **Dashboard Design:** Crafting an intuitive and visually appealing report layout.

* **Power Query ETL:** Performing data cleaning, shaping, and transformation.

* **Data Modeling:** Building efficient data models with relationships (Star Schema principles)

* **DAX Fundamentals:** Creating calculations and aggregations to derive key insights.

* **Vizualizations Utilized:**
    * **Core Charts:** Column, Bar, Line and Area charts for comparisons and trends.
    * **Map Charts:** For displaying geospatial data.
    * **Cards:** To highlight key performance indicators.
    * **Tables:** For persenting detailed, tabular information.

* **Interactive Features:**
    * **Slicers:** Enabling dynamic, user-driven data filtering.
    * **Buttons & Bookmarks:** For streamlined navigation and managing report views (including Drill-Through)

## Dashboard Overview

This second iteration consalidates the dashboard into a **single, focused page**, designed to give job seekers the most critical market insights at their fingertips.

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Dashboard_2.png)

This page acts as your concise mission control for the data job market. It showcases key performance indicators (KPIs) like **Job Count, Skills Per Job, Median Yearly Salary, And Median Hourly Salary**. You can also quickly see *Skill Popularity** (by job percent or count) and compare **Salaries across different Job Titles**, all designed for an efficient overview.

This updated dashboard (Version 2.0) showcases Power BI's ability to transform extensive job posting data into a powerful, streamlined tool for career analysis.
